How I Got Into Making Coffee

I started with instant coffee. It wasn’t fancy, but I loved the warmth and energy it gave me. Over time, I began visiting local cafes, and I really liked the vibe—the music, the atmosphere, and the people. I just knew I wanted to be part of that.

Now, coffee means more to me than just a drink. It’s a way to connect with others and share a little happiness with a great cup of coffee.
